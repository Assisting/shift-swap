Getting Started

When you first boot up Shift Swap, you are greeted by the Log In screen. Enter the username and password provided to you by your employer.

Once you've enetered your login information, press Enter to proceed. Next, you will see the main page of Shift Swap, from where all other functionality is accessed.

In the top half of the window, you can view your current schedule for the week; to see all of your currently assigned shifts for the current calendar month, click View Monthly Schedule.

The messaging system and trades are accessed via the lower left-hand corner.

Various settings pages can be accessed via the lower right-hand corner; you may not see all of these depending on the level of access set by your employer. Also here is the Help button, a shortcut to this guide.

  3. You have successfully checked your monthly schedule. To proceed, click the Back button in the upper left-hand corner.

Trade a Shift

  1. Click on the Check Available Shifts link.

  2. On the left-hand side, you'll see the shifts which other people are offering. On the right hand side, you'll see the shifts you may offer to other people.

  3. Select one of the shifts in either coloumn. You can see the details of the shift in the window at the top-centre.

  4. Click either Give Shift or Take Shift, depending on what you want to do, and what shift you've selected.

Send a Message

  1. From the front page, click Check Messages

  2. This is your inbox. If you have any messages from other employees, they will be displayed here. Click Create New Message.

  3. Here, you can specify a recipient of the message, and write the message itself. Messages are limited to 255 characters.

  4. Click Send. The message should be sent, or if there was an error, you will be notified.

Swap Shifts

  1. Click Swap Shifts.

  2. From this screen, you can search for a shift from a particular user, or on a particular date. Once you've entered the details, click Search.

  3. Now click Swap. Next, you must wait for the other employee to accept or decline your request; the message will appear in their inbox almost immediately. (A manager's approval may also be required)
