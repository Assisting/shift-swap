Manager Settings

This page gives you full access to all the most common employee management tasks. When you are logged in as a manager, it is accessible from the front page by clicking on the Manager Settings button.

Core Functions

With Shift Swap, you can:

  1. Add/Remove Employees

  2. Promote/Demote Employees

  3. Change Employee Information

  4. Change Passwords

Add Employees

  1. Click on the Add Employee tab.

  2. Enter the details as necessary and press Submit to proceed.

  3. You will be prompted to set the new employee's access level; typically, they will be a worker. Make your selection and then press Submit to proceed.

  4. Finally, set the employee's manager. Press Submit to finish adding the employee.

Remove Employees

  1. Click on the Remove Employee tab.

  2. You may search for an employee in the system by entering their first and last names, and clicking Search. The employee ID may also be filled in manually.

  3. Press Submit to remove the employee from the Shift-Swap system.

Promote/Demote Employees

  1. To change the wage and/or access level of an employee, click on the Modify Employee tab.

  2. You may search for an employee in the system by entering their first and last names, and clicking Search. The employee ID may also be filled in manually.

  3. Once the employee ID is filled in, you may enter the other requested information, and change the wage. Press Submit to proceed.

  4. You will now be asked to change the employee's access level. Make the changes required and press Submit to confirm the change.

Change Employee Information

  1. Follow the steps above in "Promote/Demote Employees" to change the employee's information as required.

  2. To change their manager, click the Set Manager tab, and fill in the information as necessary.

Change Password

  1. To change an employee's password, click the Set Password tab.

  2. Enter the employee ID. Then, enter and confirm the new password and click Submit to proceed.
